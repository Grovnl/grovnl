about us

GROV is the place where your talent in hard rock serves an equal chance to grow.   

GROV was founded in 2024 by four BUas students: Merel, Elise, Morris and Storm after they experienced some struggels in the hard-rock industry.                

GROV is the Swedish word for 'rough' which -in our opinion- relates to hard rock. We believe that hard rock fans are rough from the outside, but soft and kind from the inside.         

Vision and Mission

Vision: "In a world where popularity overshadows passion, we believe that new talent shouldn't be overlooked, because only then we can keep passion and authenticity alive." 

In our phase of research we experienced that numerous of hard rock fans are dissapointed about the fact that most of their hard rock bands are close to extinction and therefore they are not able to see their perfomances in real life. Growing as an upcoming band or as  individual hard rock artist is very hard nowadays due to lots of competition online. With Grov, we want to give every upcoming band or indivudual artist within the hard rock scene a chance to grow and shine.

Mission: "To create the new wave of hard rock legends"
