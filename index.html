How it works

For bands

Grov is the place where upcoming hard rock stars get the opportunity to show their talent. If you are an ambitious hard rock artist and believe you and your band have something worth showing to an audience, you can find your stage here. Your search for a bar, cafe or other venue to play at stops here. Filter on location to find a gig in your area. Respond to the gig and send in your availability. The owner gets an overview of all the bands that have responded to their request. They get to see your band portfolio created on your GROV account. You can add text, pictures and video to show what you got. If the bar owner likes you the most, he can book you through our website. Next thing you know, you are in front of a crowd, rocking the night away.

How it works

For venues

If you have a bar, café, or venue and want to give your customers a hard-rocking night, you can find your band here. On this platform, hard rock bars, cafes, or other venues, can send out vacancies for hard rock gigs. On your profile, you create a portfolio where you can read what kind of place you are offering. Add pictures and videos of the overall look and vibe of the place, and an estimated budget to spend on a band. Add a location to attract bands close to your area. When you find a band you like, simply book them through our website and have a blasting rock at your bar!
